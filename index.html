Against Me! Punking Gender Since 1997

Against Me! is a punk rock band founded in Florida in 1997, by lead singer and guitarist Laura Jane Grace. They have released multiple albums and toured across the country. Laura Jane Grace came out as transgender and her transgender concept album Transgender Dysphoria Blues was wildly acclaimed when it was released in 2014. Against Me! has not released a new album since 2016 but remains active and we cannot wait for what they do next!

Against Me! tour information can be found at their official site.
